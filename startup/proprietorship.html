Proprietorship Overview

What is Sole Proprietorship Registration?

A simple business structure for individuals who want to run a business with minimal compliance and full ownership control.

Proprietorship registration makes your business legally recognized and helps you access GST, PAN, and Udyam registration as required for selling goods or services.

  • Single owner business structure
  • No separate legal entity from the owner
  • Easy registration process with fewer documents
  • Ideal for freelancers, consultants, and small traders

Who can register?

  • Indian resident entrepreneur
  • Business owner with PAN and identity proof
  • Startups, shops, salons, consultants, and freelancers
  • Businesses needing GST or Udyam registration
